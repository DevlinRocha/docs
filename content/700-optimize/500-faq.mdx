## Does Optimize automatically implement optimizations

Prisma Optimize provides you with insights and recommendations on how you can improve your database queries. It does not mutate any existing queries or your Prisma schema.

## How long is a recording session retained in Optimize

There are no limits on storage retention period. Optimize will store a recording session until you explictly delete them.
