The AI Explainer feature enables you to ask follow-up questions on a provided [recommendation](/optimize/recommendation) for additional clarity.

## Using the AI Explainer

To interact with the AI Explainer:

1. Select a recommendation from an Optimize [recording](/optimize/recordings).
2. Click the **Ask AI** button.

This feature helps you gain deeper insights into a recommendation and learn how to apply it effectively to your Prisma model.
