Below are descriptions of known limitations when using Prisma Optimize. If you are aware of any limitations that are missing, please let us know on the `#help-and-questions` channel in our community [Discord](https://pris.ly/discord).

## Query limit on a recording session

Each [recording session](/optimize/recording) can have a maximum of 10K queries, after which a recording session will end.
